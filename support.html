Quick Troubleshooting

  • Camera permission If the document camera does not open, check Settings → Privacy → Camera and allow access for ScanarX.
  • Photo Library If import or save fails, check Settings → Privacy → Photos and allow access.
  • OCR or AI not available Ensure your device supports Apple Intelligence and that iOS model downloads are complete. AI features require iOS 26 for FoundationModels.
  • Export issues For CSV/PDF export failures, try a smaller export or check available storage. Exports are saved to the destination you choose in the share sheet.
  • App crashes Restart the device, reproduce the crash, then send logs and steps to support.

How to Report a Bug

Send an email to ScanarX Bug Report with:

  • Short summary of the problem
  • Steps to reproduce
  • Expected vs actual behavior
  • App version (Settings → About), device model, iOS version
  • Attach screenshots or a short screen recording

Feature Requests

We welcome suggestions. Email feature ideas to ScanarX Suggestions. Include a short use case and why it helps your workflow.

Privacy and Legal Requests

For privacy or data deletion requests, contact ScanarX Privacy. Include the device identifier shown in Settings → About if available.
