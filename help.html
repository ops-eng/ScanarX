Help

Scan

Scan Receipt
Opens the document camera, auto-crops the receipt, runs on-device OCR, parses receipt fields, stores the image and OCR text locally, then moves the receipt into the Receipts tab.
Latest Receipt
Shows the newest saved receipt as a quick card. Tap to open the receipt detail editor.
Summary tiles
Total Spend, This Month, Receipts, and Average show quick metrics for the selected period.
Privacy
Scanning, OCR, parsing, local storage, and export run on device. AI classification is on device when available and enabled.

Receipts

Total pill
Shows the sum of currently stored receipts using the selected display currency preference.
Search
Filter receipts by merchant, category, payment method, currency, notes, and OCR text.
Category chips
Tap a category chip to filter the list to receipts saved with that category.
Receipt cards
Each card shows thumbnail, merchant, date, category, total, and opens the detail editor when tapped.
Receipt details
Edit parsed fields, add notes, view raw OCR text, export PDF, share image + OCR text, or delete the receipt.

Stats

Month and year pickers
Choose a month and year for analytics. Select All to aggregate the full year.
Donut chart
Shows category share for the selected period. Center shows total spend or selected category total after selecting a slice.
Category rows
Rows list category, total, receipt count, and percentage. Tap a row to highlight and open filtered receipts.
Exports
Use Export Period to create a CSV for the selected month or year. Use Export Category to create a CSV for the selected category.
Mixed currencies
If multiple currencies are present and no exchange-rate service is enabled, Stats shows local amounts and displays a warning.

Settings

Currency
Sets the default display and fallback currency. Selection is remembered on this device.
Default Category
Used when the parser and AI classifier cannot choose a category. You can edit each receipt later.
AI
Enables on-device classification when the system model is available. Keyword parsing remains the fallback.
Export Selected Month
Creates a CSV for receipts dated inside the selected month. Path: Settings > Data > Export Selected Month.
Export All to CSV
Creates one CSV for every saved receipt. Path: Settings > Data > Export All to CSV.
Clear All Receipts
Deletes every stored receipt, image, OCR result, and saved field from this device after confirmation.

AI Status

On-device
The system model is ready. When AI is enabled, new scans use semantic on-device classification.
Enable in Settings
Apple Intelligence must be enabled in iOS Settings for on-device AI to run.
Model not ready
iOS downloads models automatically based on power, storage, network, and system conditions.
Device not eligible
Some devices do not support Apple Intelligence even on supported iOS versions.
Requires iOS 26
FoundationModels is available only on iOS 26 and later.

Export Paths

Receipt PDF
Path: Receipts > tap a receipt > Export PDF. Creates a PDF with saved fields, image, and OCR text.
Share receipt image and text
Path: Receipts > tap a receipt > Share. Opens the share sheet with image and OCR text.
Analytics CSV
Path: Stats > toolbar > Export Period or Export Category.
Tip Exported CSV files are saved to the share destination you choose. For large exports, allow time for the file to be generated.
